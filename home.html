Welcome to Our Community Service Project

PURITY in PERIL:challenges to safe drinking water

Our mission is to raise awareness about the importance of safe drinking water, assess local water quality issues, and promote simple, sustainable solutions for ensuring water safety in our community.

Why Safe Water?

Safe drinking water is essential for health, education, and economic development. Contaminated water can cause serious diseases and impact the well-being of entire communities. Our project aims to empower people with knowledge and practical solutions for water safety.

Our Mission Statement

  • Promote awareness about the importance of clean water.
  • Assess and document local water quality and supply issues.
  • Educate the community on purification and hygiene practices.
  • Encourage sustainable, community-driven solutions.
