Questionnaire

Our questionnaire covered basic awareness questions about safe drinking water facilities. Questions were asked in local languages and recorded honestly. The feedback was helpful to understand gaps in awareness.

Sample Survey Questions

  • Do you know the source of your household drinking water?
  • How often do you test or check the quality of your drinking water?
  • Are you aware of any purification methods for making water safe?
  • Have you or your family experienced waterborne illnesses in the past year?
  • What storage methods do you use for drinking water at home?
  • Would you be interested in community workshops on water safety?

How We Conducted the Survey

Our team visited households and community centers, asking questions in the local language to ensure clarity and comfort. Responses were recorded anonymously and used to identify key areas for awareness and improvement. The process was friendly, inclusive, and aimed at building trust within the community.
